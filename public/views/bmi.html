Understanding the formula for Body Mass Index (BMI)

BMI is a measure of body fat based on your weight in relation to your height, applying to most adult men and women aged 20 and over.

For children aged 2 and over, BMI percentile is the best assessment of body fat.

BMI = weight / height * height

Units: Kilograms (kg) for weight, meters (m) for height.

BMI Categories

Underweight: BMI is less than 18.5

Normal weight: BMI is 18.5 to 24.9

Overweight: BMI is 25 to 29.9

Obesity: BMI is 30 or more
